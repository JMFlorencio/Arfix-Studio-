Store Landing Page Decoration:: A Reflection of Business Credibility and Identity

By Joey Florencio | October 2025

A responsive and visually appealing online store decoration can be a great way to provide valuable insights to your target audience or potential customers. Landing pages play a crucial part in online business. In physical stores or grocery stores, all products are organized and categorized based on their categorization. In a digital business, particularly in e-commerce, a landing page should be designed based on the branding. The provided list below is the key concept structure for establishing store decoration, particularly Lazada and Shopee.

Store Header
  • Create a long banner design with your branding.
  • Incorporate a catchy tagline or phrase, encouraging users to follow for updates.
Main Banner Section
  • Design a main banner with menu links (e.g., All Products, New Arrivals, Best Sellers).
  • Highlight top products with an endorser or model to attract customers.
Voucher / Promotion Section
  • Utilize tools on platforms like Lazada and Shopee to incorporate vouchers and flash sales.
Categorization Section
  • Implement a slider or gallery for categorization.
  • Include an add-to-cart button or CTA to redirect potential buyers to the targeted product.
New Arrival Banner
  • Create a banner featuring new arrival products with clear, high-quality photos.
  • Include a CTA for customers to explore and choose their desired products.
Best Selling Product Banner
  • Showcase 3–5 top-selling products in a banner.
  • Add compelling CTAs to encourage immediate purchases.
Sponsored Affiliate Banner
  • Highlight 3–5 best-selling items with an attractive design.
  • Leverage sponsored ads on platforms like Lazada, Shopee, and TikTok to increase ROI.

In the e-commerce business, a well-designed landing page significantly influences a business’s identity and credibility. A carefully organized page with accurate categorization and highlighted products enhances user experience and influences purchasing decisions.
